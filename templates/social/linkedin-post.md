## Post Checklist

Before publishing:

- [ ] Hook grabs attention in first 2 lines
- [ ] Scenario-first framing (not product-first)
- [ ] Includes named dealership proof
- [ ] Specific metrics, not vague claims
- [ ] Soft CTA (not pushy)
- [ ] 3-5 relevant hashtags
- [ ] Character count under 3,000
- [ ] Optimal length 800-1,500
- [ ] No forbidden phrases
- [ ] Link ready (in post or for comment)
- [ ] Image prepared (1200 x 627px)

---

## Engagement Strategy

**After posting:**

1. Add link in first comment (if not in post)
2. Respond to all comments within 24 hours
3. Tag relevant connections if appropriate
4. Share to relevant LinkedIn groups

**Best posting times:**
- Tuesday-Thursday
- 7-8 AM or 12-1 PM (local time of target audience)

---

## Tone Reminders

**DO:**
- Use short sentences
- Break up text with line breaks
- Lead with scenario or statistic
- Use arrows (→) for lists
- Name specific dealerships
- Keep it conversational

**DON'T:**
- Use vendor language (leverage, streamline, optimize)
- Be salesy or pushy
- Write long paragraphs
- Overuse hashtags
- Start with "Excited to announce..."
- Lead with the product
