## Press Release Checklist

Before distributing:

- [ ] Headline is newsworthy and action-oriented
- [ ] Lead paragraph answers who/what/when/where/why
- [ ] Includes at least one executive quote
- [ ] Includes specific metrics or proof points
- [ ] Customer quote included (with permission)
- [ ] Boilerplate is current
- [ ] Contact information is accurate
- [ ] All facts verified
- [ ] No marketing hyperbole
- [ ] Embargo date clear (if applicable)
- [ ] Word count: 400-600 words

---

## Distribution Channels

1. **Wire services:** PR Newswire, Business Wire
2. **Industry publications:** Automotive News, Digital Dealer
3. **Local media:** Detroit publications (HQ)
4. **Company channels:** Website newsroom, LinkedIn, email list
5. **Trade associations:** NADA, AIADA

---

## Tone Guidelines

**DO:**
- Use factual, objective tone
- Lead with the news, not the company
- Include specific numbers and metrics
- Quote real people with real titles
- Write in third person

**DON'T:**
- Use marketing language (revolutionary, cutting-edge)
- Exaggerate claims
- Bury the lead
- Write overly long releases
- Use exclamation points
