## Quality Checklist

Before sending:

- [ ] Under 120 words total
- [ ] One pain point only (not trying to cover everything)
- [ ] Scenario-first opening (not feature-first)
- [ ] Named dealership proof point included
- [ ] Soft CTA (question format)
- [ ] No forbidden phrases (see 05-brand-voice.md)
- [ ] Subject line ≤50 characters
- [ ] Personalization tokens work ({{FirstName}}, {{Dealership}}, {{SenderName}})
- [ ] Mobile-readable (short paragraphs)
- [ ] Persona-appropriate products only (e.g., no VehicleVault to Service Manager)

## Angle-Specific Guidance

### Saturday Chaos Angle
- Open with: "It's Saturday at 11:30..."
- Pain: Lost keys, customer waiting, deal dying
- Proof: Bill Brown Ford (90% adoption, 2,000+ vehicles)
- Best for: Owner, GM, Sales Manager

### Service Revenue Angle
- Open with: Wait time scenario or cycle time stat
- Pain: Long waits, low bay turns, CSI impact
- Proof: Corwin Toyota (64% out in 60 min)
- Best for: Service Manager, GM

### Lost Key Cost Angle
- Open with: "Do you know how many keys..."
- Pain: $300-$800 per key, quiet bleeding of money
- Proof: Zero lost keys at Bill Brown Ford
- Best for: GM, Owner

### Inventory Cashflow Angle
- Open with: "Every extra day a car sits in recon..."
- Pain: Dead capital, holding costs
- Proof: Longo Toyota (3 days saved per car)
- Best for: Owner, GM, Used Car Manager

### VehicleVault Angle
- Open with: F&I revenue opportunity
- Pain: Need high-margin products, recurring revenue
- Proof: Brandon Honda ($308K/mo)
- Best for: F&I Manager, Owner, GM
- **DO NOT use for: Service Manager, Used Car Manager**
