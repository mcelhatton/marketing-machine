# Nurture Email Sequence Template

Use this template for warm leads who have shown interest but haven't booked a demo.

---

## Sequence Overview

| Email | Day | Focus |
|-------|-----|-------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Problem Awareness |
| 2 | Day 5 | Industry Trend |
| 3 | Day 10 | Solution Overview |
| 4 | Day 15 | Case Study |
| 5 | Day 20 | Demo CTA |

---

## Email 1: Problem Awareness

**Subject:** [Scenario-based pain point]

```
Hi {{FirstName}},

[SCENARIO: Describe a moment they recognize]

<!-- Example:
It's 4:30 on a Friday.
A customer wants to test drive a specific car they found online.
Nobody can find it.

The vehicle is somewhere on your lot — but where?
-->

[PAIN QUANTIFICATION: What this costs them]

<!-- Example:
That customer leaves.
Your salesperson is frustrated.
And somewhere on your lot, a $45,000 vehicle is sitting...location unknown.
-->

[SOFT BRIDGE: Hint at solution without selling]

<!-- Example:
There's a simple way to know exactly where every vehicle is — in real time.

I'll share more in my next email.
-->

— {{SenderName}}
```

## Sequence Checklist

Before launching:

- [ ] All 5 emails drafted
- [ ] Subject lines are specific, not generic
- [ ] Each email has a single focus (not trying to cover everything)
- [ ] Case study uses named dealership with specific numbers
- [ ] No forbidden phrases throughout (see 05-brand-voice.md)
- [ ] Soft CTAs (not pushy)
- [ ] Personalization tokens work
- [ ] Schedule set: Day 0 / Day 5 / Day 10 / Day 15 / Day 20
