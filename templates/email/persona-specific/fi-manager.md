# F&I Manager Email Template

Emails targeting dealership F&I Managers.

---

## Persona Characteristics

**Primary Concerns:**
- F&I revenue per vehicle
- Add-on sales
- Product penetration rates
- Recurring revenue streams
- Chargebacks

**Communication Notes:**
- Reports to GM
- Focused exclusively on finance and add-on products
- Always looking for high-margin products to sell
- Measured on F&I revenue per vehicle sold

**Note:** This persona ONLY cares about VehicleVault.

## Tone Guidance

**DO:**
- Lead with revenue and profit
- Emphasize zero chargebacks (this matters to F&I)
- Position as easy to sell (tangible product)
- Reference Brandon Honda's $308K/mo
- Mention recurring revenue

**DON'T:**
- Talk about operational benefits
- Mention other MDD products
- Get too technical about the tracking
- Forget the branded app angle (customer stickiness)

## Key Differentiators for F&I

### Why VehicleVault Sells

1. **Tangible Product**
   - Customers can touch it (physical trackers)
   - Not abstract coverage they don't understand

2. **High Perceived Value**
   - Key tracking is useful daily
   - App is dealer-branded (stickiness)
   - Rewards program included

3. **Zero Chargebacks**
   - Flexible reinsured options
   - Protects dealer margins completely

4. **Recurring Revenue**
   - Subscription income continues after the sale
   - Not a one-time sale

5. **Front or Back End**
   - Can add to addendum
   - Can sell through F&I
   - Flexible positioning
