# Service Manager Email Template

Emails targeting dealership Service Managers.

---

## Persona Characteristics

**Primary Concerns:**
- CSI scores (tied to their pay and OEM sponsorship)
- Service revenue
- Billable hours
- Cycle time / bay turns
- Employee retention

**Communication Notes:**
- Reports to GM
- Highly focused on CSI — it directly affects their compensation
- Former technicians often promoted to this role
- Busy — skim emails on phones

**Information Sources:**
- Online research (Automotive News, forums)
- Webinars, workshops on service management
- OEM requirements and benchmarks

## Tone Guidance

**DO:**
- Speak their language (RO, bay turns, cycle time, CSI)
- Reference specific time savings
- Connect to CSI (it affects their pay)
- Use Corwin Toyota as primary proof

**DON'T:**
- Pitch VehicleVault (they don't care)
- Use generic efficiency language
- Ignore the CSI connection
- Be too salesy — they're skeptical of vendors

## CSI Connection

**Always remember:** Service Managers care deeply about CSI because:
- Much of their pay plan is tied to it
- OEM sponsorship depends on it
- It's how they're measured

When writing to Service Managers, connect your message to CSI impact whenever possible.
