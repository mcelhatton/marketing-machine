# Owner/GM Email Template

Emails targeting dealership Owners and General Managers.

---

## Persona Characteristics

**Primary Concerns:**
- Company brand and reputation
- Dealership valuation
- Overall profitability
- Operational efficiency
- Employee/customer satisfaction

**Communication Notes:**
- Owner: Final decision maker
- GM: Lots of gatekeeping via phone — email often better
- Both: Prefer face-to-face for important discussions
- Both: Active on LinkedIn, follow industry trends

**Information Sources:**
- 20 Groups (NADA, NCM Associates)
- Automotive News
- Industry conferences
- Peer networks, OEM reps

---

## Tone Guidance

**DO:**
- Lead with business impact (revenue, costs, brand)
- Use peer proof (other successful dealers)
- Position as strategic partner, not vendor
- Keep it short — they're busy

**DON'T:**
- Get too technical (save that for managers)
- Use vendor language ("streamline," "optimize," "leverage")
- Be pushy — soft CTAs only
- Pitch without proof
