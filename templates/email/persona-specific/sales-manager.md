# Sales Manager Email Template

Emails targeting dealership Sales Managers.

---

## Persona Characteristics

**Primary Concerns:**
- Sales volume
- Gross profit per vehicle
- Inventory availability (when will cars be ready?)
- Team motivation
- Closing deals

**Communication Notes:**
- Reports to GM
- Primary audience for inventory status / recon updates
- Approving authority for recon work
- Motivated by deals closed and team performance

**AM Feedback:**
"Think of him as the primary audience to know what is going on with his inventory and when each will be available for sale."

## Tone Guidance

**DO:**
- Lead with the Saturday scenario (they know it well)
- Connect to deals closed / deals lost
- Emphasize team productivity (salespeople like tools that help)
- Reference Bill Brown Ford (large sales team adoption)

**DON'T:**
- Focus too much on operational details
- Ignore the VehicleVault opportunity (profit per deal)
- Be too technical — keep it about sales impact
- Use vendor language
