# Corwin Toyota Achieves 64% out in 60 minutes with Mobile Dealer Data's Service Workflow Solution

*High-Volume Toyota Dealer Transforms Operations with Real-Time Tracking Technology*

**DETROIT, MI — April 8, 2026** — Mobile Dealer Data (MDD), the leading provider of real-time vehicle and key tracking solutions for automotive dealerships, today announced that Corwin Toyota has achieved 64% out in 60 minutes following implementation of MDD's Service Workflow solution.

"With this cycle time tool, we know at the 40-minute mark if there's an issue. Before, we wouldn't know until the customer was already upset." said Service Director, Corwin Toyota at Corwin Toyota.

Corwin Toyota, high-volume toyota dealer, implemented MDD's Service Workflow solution to address operational challenges common across the automotive retail industry. The solution provides real-time visibility into vehicle locations and workflow status across all dealership properties.

**Key Results:**

- 64% out in 60 minutes
- 40-60% more bay turns
- CSI scores improved

"Corwin Toyota represents exactly the kind of operational transformation we see when dealerships commit to visibility," said a Mobile Dealer Data spokesperson. "The technology is straightforward — what matters is the results, and Corwin Toyota has achieved exceptional outcomes."

MDD's Service Workflow solution is currently deployed at over 500 dealerships nationwide, including many of the largest automotive retailers in the country.
