## Eliminate service bottlenecks.

---

### THE CHALLENGE

Express service wait times hitting 2.5 hours.

Customers leaving for quick-lube competitors. CSI scores suffering.

**The Cost:**
- Lost productivity: Hours per day spent searching
- Lost deals: Customers who won't wait
- Lost money: Holding costs adding up daily

---

### THE SOLUTION

Real-time service lane visibility that eliminates bottlenecks and improves customer satisfaction.

**How It Works:**
1. **Drop-off:** Vehicle automatically checked in as it enters the service lane.
2. **Track:** Real-time location and status through every stage of service.
3. **Alert:** Proactive notifications when vehicles exceed target times.

**Key Features:**
- **Real-Time Service Bay Visibility:** See every vehicle's location and status from advisor desks or any device. Know instantly what's in progress, what's waiting, what's done.
- **Cycle Time Tracking:** Automatic timestamps at every stage. Identify bottlenecks before they become customer complaints.
- **Proactive Alert System:** Get notified when vehicles exceed target cycle times. Intervene early instead of apologizing later.
- **Technician Efficiency:** Eliminate time techs spend walking the lot looking for their next job. Vehicles come to them in sequence.
