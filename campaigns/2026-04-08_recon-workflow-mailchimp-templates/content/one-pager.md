### THE SOLUTION

Real-time reconditioning visibility that cuts days from your time-to-line.

**How It Works:**
1. **Intake:** Vehicle enters system at acquisition. Timer starts.
2. **Track:** Real-time status through every recon stage and vendor.
3. **Optimize:** Analytics identify bottlenecks. Accountability drives improvement.

**Key Features:**
- **Acquisition to Front-Line Tracking:** See every vehicle's status from trade-in or auction through inspection, reconditioning, photos, and front-line ready.
- **Bottleneck Identification:** Instantly see which stage is slowing you down. Is it PDI? Detail? Photos? Know in real-time, not after the fact.
- **Vendor Accountability:** Track vehicles at off-site vendors. Know exactly how long that body shop has had your car.
- **Cost Per Day Tracking:** See holding costs accumulating in real-time. Every day a car sits is cash not in your pocket.
