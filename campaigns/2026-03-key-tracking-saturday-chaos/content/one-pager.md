## Find any key in seconds.

### THE CHALLENGE

It's Saturday at 11:30am. A customer is ready to buy. Nobody can find the key.

Three employees searching the lot. Customer waiting awkwardly. Deal momentum dying.

**The Cost:**
- Lost productivity: Hours per day spent searching
- Lost deals: Customers who won't wait
- Lost money: $200-500 per lost key

---

### THE SOLUTION

Real-time key tracking that eliminates the chaos of lost keys and wasted time.

**How It Works:**
1. **Tag:** Every key gets a Bluetooth tracker. Attach in seconds.
2. **Track:** Your team sees every key's real-time location from their phone.
3. **Find:** Use warmer/colder to walk directly to any key instantly.

**Key Features:**
- **Real-Time Key Location:** See exactly where every key is — on a desk, in a pocket, across the lot. No more key boxes that are only as good as the last person.
- **Warmer/Colder Finding:** Walk directly to any key using proximity guidance on your phone. No more systematic searches through drawers and desks.
- **Key Accountability Tracking:** See who last checked out a key and where it went. Eliminate finger-pointing and build real accountability.
- **Zero Behavior Change:** No check-in, no check-out, no scanning. The system tracks automatically so your team can focus on selling.
