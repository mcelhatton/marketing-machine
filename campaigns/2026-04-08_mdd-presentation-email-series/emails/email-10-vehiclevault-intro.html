What is VehicleVault?

A consumer key tracking product sold through F&I. Customers get two Bluetooth trackers for their key fobs and a dealer-branded app. Every time they open it to find their keys, they see your dealership name.

For the customer:

  • Two Bluetooth key trackers
  • Real-time key location via app
  • Vehicle info management (VIN, mileage, docs)
  • Rewards & loyalty program
  • Something tangible they can touch and use

For the dealership:

  • High-margin add-on revenue
  • Recurring subscription income
  • Zero chargebacks (flexible reinsured options)
  • Customer stickiness (your brand on their phone)
  • Front-end or back-end positioning
