How It Works

Tag. Track. Manage.

Every vehicle and key gets a Bluetooth tracking device the moment it enters inventory. Your team can instantly pinpoint any key or car — from their phone.

1

Tag

Attach Bluetooth tracker to vehicle & key. Scan the QR code, assign to VIN — done in 10 seconds.

2

Track

Real-time location via mobile app. Search by VIN, stock number, or name. Find any key or car instantly.

3

Manage

Run audits, get movement alerts, see who last had a key. Full accountability and visibility from anywhere.

Key capabilities:

✓ Real-time location for every key and vehicle
✓ After-hours movement alerts and geofencing
✓ DMS integration (Reynolds & Reynolds, CDK)
✓ Full accountability and audit trail

Note: This is Bluetooth-based tracking — it works better indoors where your cars actually are. Not GPS.
