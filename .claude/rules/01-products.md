## MDD Locate

**Category:** Key & Vehicle Tracking (Flagship Product)

**One-Sentence Description:** Real-time Bluetooth tracking that shows your team exactly where every key and car is on your lot — instantly.

**How It Works:**
1. **Tag** — Every vehicle and key gets a Bluetooth tracker. Scan the QR code, assign to stock number — done in 10 seconds.
2. **Track** — Staff sees every key and car's real-time location from their phone or desktop.
3. **Manage** — Run audits, get movement alerts, see who last had a key, integrate with your DMS.

**Key Features:**
- Real-time key location (on a desk, in a pocket, across the lot)
- Vehicle location across all lots (multiple properties supported)
- After-hours movement alerts (geofenced areas, off-hours notifications)
- DMS Integration (Reynolds & Reynolds, CDK — stock numbers, VINs auto-populate)
- Daily audit tools (instant location verification without walking the lot)
- Key accountability tracking (see who last checked out a key)

**Benefits by Persona:**
| Persona | Key Benefit |
|---------|-------------|
| Owner | Protect brand, reduce customer frustration, lower lost key costs |
| GM | Operational efficiency, stop losing customer opportunities |
| Sales Manager | Close deals faster, reduce employee frustration |
| Service Manager | Locate cars/keys when ready to work, reduce lost time |
| Used Car Manager | Find keys instantly, close deals before customer leaves |

**Problem It Solves (Dealer Language):**
"It's Saturday at 11:30. A customer is ready to buy. Your salesperson can't find the key. Now 3 employees are searching the lot while the customer waits awkwardly."

**Important Notes:**
- Hardware is Bluetooth-based (like AirTags), NOT GPS
- Bluetooth works better indoors where dealership cars often are
- Key lock boxes only create accountability — "only as good as the last person that took the key"
- This is NOT an anti-theft device

## VehicleVault

**Category:** F&I Consumer Product (Revenue Generator)

**One-Sentence Description:** A high-margin F&I product that customers actually use — key tracking for them, recurring profit for you, zero chargebacks.

**How It Works:**
1. F&I team offers VehicleVault at the desk during the sale
2. Customer gets two Bluetooth trackers for their key fobs
3. Customer downloads dealer-branded app to track keys, manage vehicle info, earn rewards
4. Dealership gets high-margin revenue with zero chargebacks

**Key Features:**
- **For the Customer:**
  - Two Bluetooth key trackers
  - Dealer-branded mobile app
  - Real-time key location
  - Vehicle info management (VIN, mileage, registration, documents, service history)
  - Rewards & loyalty program (loyalty dollars redeemable at dealership)

- **For the Dealership:**
  - High-margin add-on revenue
  - Recurring subscription income
  - Zero chargebacks (flexible reinsured options)
  - Customer stickiness (your brand on their phone)
  - Front-end or back-end positioning (add to addendum OR sell through F&I)

**Benefits by Persona:**
| Persona | Key Benefit |
|---------|-------------|
| Owner | Recurring revenue, high profit per deal |
| GM | Incremental revenue, customer retention |
| Sales Manager | Drive incremental revenue per car sold, front or back end gross |
| F&I Manager | PRIMARY — High margin, recurring revenue, zero chargebacks, easy to sell |
| Service Manager | DO NOT PITCH — Won't care until service communication is integrated |
| Used Car Manager | DO NOT PITCH — Likely won't care |

**Important Notes:**
- This is an F&I product SOLD TO CUSTOMERS, not an internal tool
- Every time customer opens the app to find their keys, they see YOUR dealership name
- Tangible product customers can touch and understand (not abstract coverage)
- "Vehicle Vault" in older materials should be written as "VehicleVault"
