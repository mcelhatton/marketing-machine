## Owner (Execs Segment)

*If not part of a PE or Auto Group*

### Demographics
- **Education:** Bachelor's Degree
- **Gender:** Male
- **Age:** 55+
- **Org Size:** 20-100 employees

### Reports To
N/A — Final authority

### Communication Preferences
- Email: Yes
- Phone: Yes
- Face-to-Face: Preferred for negotiations and important discussions
- Social: LinkedIn (engages with industry trends, joins relevant groups)

### Key Responsibilities
- **People Management:** Oversees leadership team. Usually defers to GM unless "Owner/Operator"
- **Financial Decision-Making:** Final decision maker on large purchases
- **Face of the Dealership:** Sets tone, holds leadership accountable through GM and FOD

### KPIs They're Measured By
- Total Sales Volume
- Gross Profit per Vehicle
- Days' Supply of Inventory
- Inventory Turnover Ratio
- F&I Revenue per Vehicle Sold
- Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT)
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Service Revenue
- Customer Retention Rate
- Cost per Sale
- Lead Conversion Rate

### Primary Goals
**AM Correction:** "Most concerned about the company brand, and value of the dealership."

- Increase Revenue (car sales AND service sales)
- Improve Operational Efficiency
- Enhance Customer Experience
- Attract and Retain Talent

### How They Gain Information
**AM Correction:** "You will find they chase industry news, upcoming trends etc, rather than management skill development."

- **Networking:** 20 Groups (peer advisory boards — NADA and NCM Associates are popular moderators)
- **Online Research:** Automotive News, industry forums
- **Industry Events:** Conferences and trade shows
- **Professional Development:** Advisory boards, seminars

### Common Challenges
- Market Competition (staying ahead in saturated market)
- Employee Turnover
- Technological Adaptation

### MDD Product Benefits
| Product | Key Benefits |
|---------|--------------|
| Key & Car Locate | Reduce employee/customer frustration (brand concern), stop losing customer opportunities, lower lost key costs |
| Service Workflow | Increase service turns, more billable hours |
| Recon Workflow | More efficient recon, higher inventory turns, lower holding costs |
| VehicleVault | High profit, incremental recurring revenue |
