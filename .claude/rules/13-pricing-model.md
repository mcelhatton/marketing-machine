# MDD Pricing Model

Pricing information for internal reference. Use carefully in marketing content.

---

## Important Note

**Pricing is customized per dealership.**

The figures below are general ranges for use in sales materials. Always drive to demo/consult for specific pricing.

---

## Pricing Structure

### Installation
- **One-time fee**
- Varies by dealership size and configuration
- Each dealership is different

### Base Subscription
- Monthly base amount
- Covers platform access and core features

### Workflow Add-Ons
- **Service Workflow:** ~$400/month
- **Recon Workflow:** ~$400/month
- Can add one or both

### Per-Unit Fees
- **Per key:** ~$3/month
- **Per car:** ~$3/month

## Pricing Language for Content

### DO Use:
- "Starting at $55 per vehicle"
- "Custom pricing based on your dealership's needs"
- "No long-term contracts"
- "No hidden fees"
- "Schedule a demo for pricing details"

### DON'T Use:
- Exact per-key or per-car fees in public content
- Internal pricing formulas
- "See our pricing page" (we don't have one)
- Specific installation costs

## Value Framing

### ROI Positioning
Instead of leading with price, lead with value:

**Lost Keys:**
> Replacing a modern key fob costs $300-$800. How many does your dealership lose per year?

**Time Savings:**
> If your team spends 30 minutes per day searching for keys and cars, that's 180+ hours per year.

**Service Revenue:**
> Corwin Toyota increased bay turns 40-60%. How much additional revenue would that mean for your service department?

**F&I Revenue (VehicleVault):**
> Brandon Honda generates $308K/mo in additional F&I revenue. What would even a fraction of that mean for your dealership?

## Pricing Objection Handling

### "It's too expensive"

Response framework:
1. Acknowledge the concern
2. Quantify the current problem cost
3. Compare to MDD investment
4. Reference ROI from named dealership

Example:
```
I understand budget is a consideration.

Quick question: How much does your dealership spend on lost key replacements each year? At $300-$800 per key, it adds up fast.

Bill Brown Ford eliminated lost keys entirely. The system often pays for itself in the first month.
```

### "We're too small"

Response:
```
MDD works for dealerships of all sizes — from single-point stores to the largest publicly traded groups.

The technology is the same that Bill Brown Ford uses to track 2,000+ vehicles. We just scale the pricing to fit your operation.
```
