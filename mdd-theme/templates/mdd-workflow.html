Common Questions

Everything you need to know about LocateIQ workflows.

How does automatic updating work?

Because MDD tracks the physical location of every vehicle via Bluetooth, when a car moves from one zone to another, the workflow advances automatically. No manual status updates needed.

Can we customize the workflow stages?

Absolutely. Define your own stages for service or recon: inspection, body, mechanical, detail, photo, front-line — whatever matches your process.

What’s IP vs. NIP tracking?

In-Process vs. Not-In-Process. MDD is the only system that tracks time during operational hours only. Your metrics are actionable and credible, not inflated by overnight hours.

How do bottleneck alerts work?

Set thresholds for time-in-stage. When a vehicle exceeds the threshold, you get an alert. No more discovering problems after the fact.

Does this replace our existing workflow software?

MDD can work alongside or replace existing tools. The key difference: MDD adds physical location intelligence that other tools simply can’t provide.

What about vendor accountability?

Time-based metrics show exactly how long each vendor takes. Hold outside detailers, body shops, and other vendors accountable with real data.
