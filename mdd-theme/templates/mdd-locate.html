MDD uses Bluetooth technology that provides sub-foot accuracy indoors and across your lot. Unlike GPS, Bluetooth works perfectly inside service bays, detail shops, and covered areas.

Most dealerships are fully operational within a day. Each tag takes about 10 seconds to attach. We handle the configuration and training.

Yes. MDD integrates with Reynolds & Reynolds, CDK, and other major DMS platforms. Stock numbers and VINs auto-populate.
