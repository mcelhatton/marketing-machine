## Company Overview

Mobile Dealer Data provides Auto Dealers with Car and Key tracking. They attach Bluetooth-driven tags (similar to AirTags) to every key and vehicle. Tags have QR codes that get scanned in the app and assigned to the car — this syncs to the dealer's inventory automatically.

Staff can see exactly where every key or car is at all times via mobile app or desktop.

**Why It Matters:**
- Saturday hits and everyone is busy — they lose track quickly
- Key lock box systems only create accountability — "only as good as the last person that took the key"
- They don't work well

**Products:**
1. **MDD Locate** — Key and car tracking
2. **Service Workflow** — Automated workflow tracking for service department
3. **Recon Workflow** — Automated workflow tracking for reconditioning
4. **VehicleVault** — F&I product customers buy with the car (two trackers for their keys + dealer-branded app)

**Key Differentiator:** LocateIQ
- Pronounced "locate IQ"
- Powers Service Workflow and Recon Workflow
- Because MDD knows where the car physically is, workflows can be automated
- System can set triggers to move the car in the system when the physical car has moved
- More accurate and automated vs. someone manually keying updates

## Competitors

- **TrueSpot** — Closest competitor on location/tracking
- **LoJack** — GPS-based solution (doesn't work well indoors where dealership cars often are)
- **Rapid Recon** — Recon workflow competitor
- **Key lock box systems** — Legacy approach (only creates accountability, not location)
