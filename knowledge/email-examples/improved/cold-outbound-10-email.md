## Why These Work

1. **Scenario-first:** "Saturday at 11:30" — they recognize it instantly
2. **Specific proof:** Named dealerships with real numbers
3. **Short:** Under 120 words each
4. **One pain per email:** Focused, not overwhelming
5. **Soft CTAs:** "Worth seeing?" not "Book now!"
6. **Curiosity:** Questions that make them think
