## Why These Underperform

**Problems identified:**

1. **Vendor brochure language:**
   - "Streamline operations"
   - "Cutting-edge tracking solution"
   - "Enhance customer experiences"
   - "Unlock new levels"

2. **Generic benefits:**
   - No specific scenarios
   - No named dealership proof
   - No specific numbers

3. **Hard CTAs:**
   - "Sign up for a quick demo today!"
   - "Book a demo now!"

4. **Too long:**
   - Each email has too much copy
   - Dealership operators skim on phones
