## Why These Underperform

1. **Vendor language:** "Revolutionize," "cutting-edge," "streamline," "innovative"

2. **No cash flow angle:** Should lead with "A $40K car sitting in recon is cash sitting on the lot"

3. **No proof points:** Should reference Longo Toyota's 3 days saved per car in recon

4. **Missing LocateIQ mechanism:** Doesn't explain WHY MDD's recon workflow is different (auto-advances based on physical location)

5. **No bottleneck language:** Should talk about identifying where cars are getting stuck

6. **Generic efficiency claims:** "Enhance efficiency" means nothing — should say "35% reduction in days to frontline"
