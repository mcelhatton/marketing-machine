## Why These Underperform

1. **Vendor language throughout:** "Unlock your sales potential," "Streamline sales operations," "Empower your sales team"

2. **No Saturday scenario:** Should open with "It's Saturday. Customer ready to buy. No one can find the key."

3. **No proof points:** Should reference Bill Brown Ford's 90% sales team adoption

4. **Missing recon angle:** Sales Managers care about inventory status and when cars will be available — these emails don't address Recon Workflow

5. **Generic CTAs:** Should use soft CTAs like "Worth seeing?" instead of "Book your demo now!"
