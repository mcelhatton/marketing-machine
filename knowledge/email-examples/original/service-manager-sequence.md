## Why These Underperform

1. **Generic language:** "Streamline," "enhance," "unlock" — these are vendor phrases that every software company uses

2. **No scenario-first opening:** Should lead with "Techs standing around waiting for keys" not "Transform your service operations"

3. **No proof points:** Should reference Corwin Toyota's 64% out in 60 minutes

4. **Misses CSI connection:** Service managers care about CSI scores because it affects their pay — these emails don't address that

5. **Too pushy:** "Don't miss out—book your demo today!" feels like sales pressure
