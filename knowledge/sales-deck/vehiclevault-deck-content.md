## Slide 3: How Does It Work?

Every vehicle and key is tagged with a Bluetooth tracking device the moment it enters inventory. Dealership staff can instantly pinpoint the location of any vehicle or key directly from their mobile app — eliminating guesswork and saving valuable time.

### The Flow:
1. **Tag** — Attach Bluetooth tracker to vehicle & key
2. **Track** — Real-time location via mobile app
3. **Manage** — Audit, locate & organize from anywhere
