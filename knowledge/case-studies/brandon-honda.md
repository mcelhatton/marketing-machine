## The Challenge

Brandon Honda faced multiple operational challenges:

1. **Lost Keys**
   - Keys going missing regularly
   - Time wasted searching
   - Customer frustration

2. **Service Efficiency**
   - Time lost per repair order
   - No visibility into process bottlenecks

3. **F&I Revenue**
   - Looking for high-margin add-on products
   - Need for recurring revenue streams
   - Wanted products with zero chargebacks

---

## The Solution

Brandon Honda implemented the full MDD platform:
- Key and vehicle tracking (MDD Locate)
- Service workflow automation
- **VehicleVault F&I product**

VehicleVault became a key differentiator — a high-margin F&I product they offer to every customer at the desk.
