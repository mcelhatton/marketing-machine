## The Challenge

Corwin Toyota's express service department was underperforming:

**The Problem:**
- Express service wait times stretched to **2.5 hours**
- Customers were frustrated: "No one wants to wait two hours for an oil change"
- At risk of losing customer-pay business to quick-lube competitors
- No visibility into where bottlenecks were occurring
- No real-time way to identify vehicles stuck in the process

The traditional approach — someone manually logging status changes — wasn't working. Staff were too busy to update the system, so the workflow dashboard didn't reflect reality.

---

## The Solution

Corwin Toyota implemented Mobile Dealer Data's:
- **Cycle Time Reduction** tools
- **Automated Service Workflow** powered by LocateIQ

**How LocateIQ Made the Difference:**
Because MDD tracks the physical location of every vehicle, the workflow updates automatically when cars move. No manual status updates required.

Warren's team now knows at the 40-minute mark whether there's an issue with a particular delivery, and can get help there immediately.
