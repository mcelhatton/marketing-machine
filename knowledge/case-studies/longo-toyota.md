## The Challenge

Longo Toyota faced significant time waste in two areas:

1. **Vehicle Location Time**
   - Finding vehicles took up to 25 minutes
   - Staff walking lots, searching for cars
   - Delays in getting vehicles to customers

2. **Reconditioning Cycle**
   - Days lost in the recon process
   - No visibility into where bottlenecks occurred
   - Cars sitting longer than necessary

---

## The Solution

Longo Toyota implemented Mobile Dealer Data's:
- Real-time vehicle and key tracking
- Automated workflow for reconditioning
- Instant location via mobile app

---

## Calculating the Impact

### Repair Orders
If Longo Toyota processes 50 ROs per day:
- 15 min saved × 50 ROs = **750 minutes saved daily**
- That's **12.5 hours** of recovered productivity per day

### Reconditioning
If they recon 100 cars per month:
- 3 days saved × 100 cars = **300 days** of reduced holding time
- At ~$40 holding cost per day = **$12,000/month** in reduced costs
