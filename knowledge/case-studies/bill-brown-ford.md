## The Challenge

Bill Brown Ford manages thousands of vehicles across multiple properties:
- Two adjacent lots (800 units each)
- Additional lots with 1,000 and 1,700 cars
- Used car operation with 500 vehicles
- Keys constantly moving between sales, service, and recon

**The Problem:**
Previously relied on manual data entry from their DMS, which often led to confusion. Cars were misplaced or misidentified, wasting valuable time.

When it came to finding keys, the situation was even worse. Staff spent hours searching for keys that had ended up on desks, in pockets, or across the lot.

---

## The Solution

Bill Brown Ford implemented Mobile Dealer Data's pinpoint tracking for:
- Vehicle keys
- Vehicle locations
- Full DMS integration with Reynolds & Reynolds

**Technical Implementation:**
- Bluetooth trackers on every key and vehicle
- Integration with key keeper systems
- Mobile app deployment to sales team
- Customizable alerts for movement, battery, and inactivity

---

## The Results

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Vehicles tracked | 2,000+ across multiple lots |
| Sales team adoption | 90% of 29-person new car sales team |
| Key/vehicle location | Instant, pinpoint accuracy |
| Time saved | Eliminated hours of searching weekly |

### Specific Improvements:
- Instantly locate any of 2,000+ vehicles
- Prepare vehicles for delivery without delays
- Verify if keys are in the key control system or with an individual
- Track unauthorized vehicle movements
- Receive alerts for low battery, prolonged inactivity, after-hours movement
