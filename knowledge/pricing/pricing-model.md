## Value Positioning

### Cost Justification Framework

**Lost Keys:**
- Modern key replacement: $300-$800 per key
- If a dealership loses 2-3 keys per month: $600-$2,400/month
- MDD eliminates this cost entirely

**Lost Time (Keys):**
- If staff spend 30 min/day searching for keys
- 30 min × $25/hour × 22 days = $275/month in labor
- × multiple staff members = significant cost

**Lost Deals:**
- Customer walks because of wait time
- Value of one lost deal >> annual MDD cost

**Service Revenue (Corwin Toyota):**
- Bay turns increased 40-60%
- 64% of customers out in 60 minutes vs. 2.5 hours
- More throughput = more revenue

**Recon Holding Costs:**
- $40-50 per day per vehicle in holding costs
- 3 days saved (Longo Toyota) × $45 × 100 cars = $13,500/month

**VehicleVault Revenue:**
- Brandon Honda: $308K/month additional F&I revenue
- Zero chargebacks
- Recurring subscription income
